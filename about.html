About Me

Welcome to DailyDriverss!

My name is Liam and I set up DailyDriverss.com in 2020 to provide consumers and car enthusiasts with a friendly, fun and honest viewpoint on cars and the motor industry. I have been reviewing new cars and writing about the industry for the past 3 years.

I’ve always been in love with the automobile. I was the kid who knew every car brand and model. But I never grew up wanting to be a motoring journalist. For sure I read all the magazines but I never thought that motoring writing was a career open to me.

Now I’m proud to be a freelance motoring journalist and car blogger, also dabbling in some automotive photography and video presenting. I’m the founder and editor of one of Ireland’s best car blogs. I’ve driven more cars than I can remember (triple digits now). I’ve written hundreds of car reviews, features and news stories. I’ve been published in some leading Irish newspapers and on influential international websites.

I’ve bumbled around the Frankfurt Motor Show and the Paris Motor Show on a press pass for Changing Lanes. I’ve travelled to the UK, France, Netherlands, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Croatia and Greece to test drive cars. I’ve made more than a handful of car videos. I’ve built up a loyal following of car fans across Twitter, Facebook, YouTube and Instagram.

I know my ABS from my ESP and I know a good car from a bad car. The best car I ever tested was the Ford Focus RS.

The worst car? That’s another story… I hope you enjoy this site and please follow me on social media so you never miss an update!

We are are also on updating and posting constantly on social media, follow us!!
